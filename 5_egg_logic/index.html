Two-Egg Strategy — Optimal Plan (100 Floors)

Goal: minimize the maximum number of drops in the worst case. With two eggs, the optimal plan uses decreasing steps so total worst-case drops ≤ 14.

Why 14? Choose the first-egg steps as t, t−1, t−2, …, 1 so they cover at least 100 floors:
t(t+1)/2 ≥ 100 ⇒ t = 14 (since 14·15/2 = 105).
Drop at floors: 14, 27, 39, 50, 60, 69, 77, 84, 90, 95, 99, 100.
